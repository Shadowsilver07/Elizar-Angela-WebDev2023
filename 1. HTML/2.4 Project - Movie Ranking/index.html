My top 3 movies of all-time

Lord Of The Rings

This has trilogies and beginnings, story of Humans, Elves, Dwarves, Hobbits and Orcs -- 4 races that became united to fight the tyranny of the vicious orcs and Sauron who wants to rule the whole Middle-Earth.

Godzilla

An ancient giant lizard, that was mutated by accident by radio active substance during world war and became the most dominating Kaiju living whose origin was about to unfold. He was awaken since other monsters revival who live during ancient times and coming back to dominate again the earth. This is an ongoing movie and the last Episode was Godzilla vs Kong.

Avengers

The team assembled to fight the threats to humanity and universe, the team include Hulk, Thor, HawkEye, Captain America, Black Widow, Iron Man and the other members. Each has his own abilities and comprises of different race, they were assembled to fight for justice, freedom and peace
